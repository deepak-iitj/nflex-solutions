# Category tile images

These images are used in the "Browse by Category" section on the home page.

- **plc.svg** – PLCs & Controllers  
- **switchgear.svg** – Switchgear & Panels  
- **sensors.svg** – Sensors & Instruments  
- **drives.svg** – Drives & Motors  
- **safety.svg** – Safety Systems  
- **cables.svg** – Cables & Connectors  

You can replace any `.svg` with a `.jpg` or `.png` of the same name (e.g. `plc.jpg`) and update the `image` path in `src/pages/Index.tsx` if the extension changes.
